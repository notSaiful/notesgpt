Data Privacy

Your Data.
Your Control.

Concise, transparent rules about how your information is handled.

1. Information We Collect

NotesGPT acts as an interface bridging your educational queries and AI generation. We do not require accounts, logins, or personal identifying information to use the primary generator features. We only process the topic inputs and subject selections you submit to generate your notes.

2. How We Use Information

Data provided in the form is used exclusively for the real-time API generation of study materials. We do not sell, rent, or trade your interaction logs to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We utilize cutting-edge AI services (like OpenRouter and Bytez) to power the knowledge generation. When you request a note, your specific, anonymous prompt is sent to these services. They process the text securely according to their own robust AI privacy standards.

4. Local Storage

Any study history or saved flashcards are persisted securely in your browser's local storage. This means your data never leaves your device and remains completely within your control. You can clear your browser data at any time to wipe your NotesGPT history.

5. Updates

We may update this policy occasionally as we add new features. Because we don't hold your email, please check this page for any future changes regarding data handling.
