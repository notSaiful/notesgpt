Why we built NotesGPT

We built NotesGPT because the modern educational framework wastes too much student time. Reading 50 pages of textbook fluff to understand a 5-minute core concept should not be the standard, but unfortunately, it is.

We believe learning should be direct, targeted, and active. Instead of passive, hours-long reading sessions, NotesGPT acts as a distillation engine. We extract the undeniable core facts, crucial formulas, and exam-focused points from dense material.

"Stop deciphering paragraphs and start mastering concepts."

The Process

By leveraging advanced AI models, we map specific subjects and topics to a neural-based analysis structure tailored strictly to CBSE standards. The output? Zero filler. Just exactly what you need to know, paired with instantaneous flashcards to cement your understanding.
